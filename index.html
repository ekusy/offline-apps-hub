How to Play Offline

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap on any game to play it
  2. Look for the "Download for Offline" button in the game
  3. Tap it to save the game to your device
  4. Play offline anytime!

iOS (Safari)

  1. Tap on any game to play it
  2. Look for the "Download for Offline" button in the game
  3. Follow the instructions to add to home screen
  4. Play offline anytime!
